Iceland

Land of ice and fire, Iceland is perfect for those who enjoy epic scenery and true serenity. Enjoy this 9-day Ring Road itinerary, designed for those who plan on renting a car.
Day Agenda
1 Land in Keflavik airport, where you'll pick up your rental car. After relaxing in the Blue Lagoon, spend the day exploring Reykjavik.
2 Start your journey along Iceland's Ring Road by heading clockwise. Enjoy the views around the Snæfellsnes peninsula, taking a pitstop to snap an iconic picture at Kirkjufell
3 Continue journeying on the Ring Road, heading northeast. Marvel at spectacular scenery Tröllaskagi before spending the night in Siglufjörður.
4 Explore Akureykri (Iceland's second-largest city) before visiting Goðafoss' falls and whale watching in Húsavík.
5 Drive through Vatnajökull National Park, exploring Asbyrgyi Canyon and Dettifoss along the way.
6 Spend the day touring Myvatn before passing through Hveragerði's geothermal vents on your way southeast.
7 Grab chunks of glacier at Jökulsárlón before viewing the epic Fjaðrárgljúfur Canyon.
8 Take a walk on the black sand beach at Vik, then take your #selfie in front of iconic Seljalandsfoss.
9 Hustle with tourists along Iceland's famous Golden Circle, a trio of must-see sights: Þingvellir National Park, Gullfoss, and Geysir. End your day back in Reykjavik before an early flight home the next day.
